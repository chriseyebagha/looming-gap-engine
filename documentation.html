Looming Gap Engine

System intelligence & product overview

Documentation v1.1

User Perspective

The Looming Gap Engine is a personalized up-skilling co-pilot designed to automate the trajectory of your professional growth by identifying skill gaps directly from the market.

Concept

Instead of guessing what technologies to master, the engine uses real-world job data to quantify demand. It's a bridge between the jobs you want and the skills you need.

Core Workflow

  • Mobile Capture: Share job links directly into your Notion Mobile Inbox. The system handles the rest.
  • Intelligence Engine: Periodically analyzes JDs, extracts technical requirements, and cross-references your current profile.
  • Dynamic Prioritization: Automatically updates your Master Skill Tracker, surfacing high-frequency gaps as learning priorities.
  • Daily Delivery: Every morning at 08:00, a curated brief arrives in Apple Notes with targeted learning resources.

Strategic Value

  • Precision Learning: Focus only on what the market is actively asking for.
  • Mobile Integrated: Capture high-value signals instantly from your phone while browsing.
  • Automated Research: High-signal resources are discovered and delivered daily.

Engineer Perspective

Stack & Integration

A headless automation suite built on Node.js with deep system hooks:

  • Notion API: Primary data lake for job pipelines and skill metrics.
  • Puppeteer: Programmatic browser automation for scraping dynamic JDs.
  • Dev.to API: Source for community-vetted, high-signal technical content.
  • AppleScript: Native execution bridge for macOS app interaction.
  • launchd: System-level lifecycle management and persistent orchestration.

System Architecture

  • upskill.js: The primary ingestion engine. Handles scraping and regex-based skill extraction.
  • morning_brief.js: The aggregation layer. Compiles intelligence and triggers delivery.
  • skill_scheduler.js: State management for the learning queue.

Automation Lifecycle

Scheduled via macOS Launch Agents (~/Library/LaunchAgents/):

  • Watcher: Persistent polling (every 6h + 18:00 daily).
  • Brief: Scheduled execution (daily at 08:00).
